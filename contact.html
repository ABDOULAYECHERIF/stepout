Our Journey

Stepout was founded by me and my mother, driven by our love for travel and the desire to make it easy and joyful for others. From group adventures to solo trips—including my first one at age 10 in Austria—we’ve explored the world with curious hearts and open minds.

Before creating Stepout, I gained firsthand experience working with other travel companies. These insights taught me how to craft seamless journeys and tailor unforgettable experiences for all types of explorers.
