Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a trip with Step Out Travels?

You can book directly through our website, by calling our office, or by sending us a message through this contact form. Our team will guide you through the entire booking process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and in some cases, payment plans can be arranged for certain trips.

Are your trips halal-friendly?

Yes! All our trips are designed with halal-friendly considerations including food options, prayer facilities, and culturally appropriate accommodations.

Can I customize my trip?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized travel experiences. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and preferences.
