Discover Lisbon's Soul

This 6-day escape to Lisbon is designed to immerse you in the city's vibrant culture, stunning architecture, and delicious cuisine. From the moment you arrive, you'll feel the warm Portuguese sun and the welcoming spirit of this historic yet modern capital.

Lisbon is a city of contrasts - ancient trams rattle past contemporary art galleries, traditional Fado music echoes from tiny taverns while rooftop bars pulse with modern beats. Our carefully curated itinerary balances must-see sights with hidden gems, group activities with free time to explore at your own pace.

Trip Highlights

Private guided tour of Alfama, Lisbon's oldest district
Sunset cruise on the Tagus River with local wine
Pastel de nata baking workshop with a local chef
Authentic Fado music night in a traditional tavern
Day trip to Sintra's fairytale palaces and forests

Daily Itinerary

Your Lisbon adventure day by day

Day 1: Welcome to Lisbon

  • Arrival at Lisbon Airport, private transfer to hotel
  • Welcome meeting with your guide and group
  • Evening walking tour of Baixa district
  • Welcome dinner at a traditional Portuguese restaurant

Day 2: Alfama & Belém

  • Morning guided tour of Alfama's winding streets
  • Visit to São Jorge Castle with panoramic views
  • Afternoon exploring Belém's monuments
  • Taste the original Pastel de Belém at Fábrica dos Pastéis

Day 3: Sintra Day Trip

  • Full-day excursion to magical Sintra
  • Visit Pena Palace and its colorful gardens
  • Explore Quinta da Regaleira's mystical wells
  • Evening at leisure to explore Lisbon nightlife

Day 4: Culinary Lisbon

  • Morning Pastel de Nata baking workshop
  • Visit to Mercado da Ribeira food market
  • Free afternoon for shopping or exploring
  • Sunset cruise on the Tagus River with wine

Day 5: LX Factory & Fado Night

  • Morning visit to LX Factory creative hub
  • Free time for last-minute discoveries
  • Farewell dinner with authentic Fado performance

Day 6: Departure

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Private transfer to airport
  • Bid farewell to Lisbon and your new friends
